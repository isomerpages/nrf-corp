<p>The&nbsp;<strong>Singapore-Horizon Europe (SG-HE) Complementary Fund&nbsp;</strong>will
support research or activities conducted in Singapore by researchers from
eligible Singapore entities, as part of Horizon Europe projects in priority
areas for Singapore.</p>
<p>NRF has organised the SG-HE Complementary Fund Information Sessions on
25 and 26 March 2026. Please refer to the following links for the <a href="https://go.gov.sg/sg-horizon-info-sessions" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">Information Session Slides</a> and
