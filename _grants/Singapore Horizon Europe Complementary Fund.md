<p>The&nbsp;<strong>Singapore-Horizon Europe (SG-HE) Complementary Fund&nbsp;</strong>will
support research or activities conducted in Singapore by researchers from
eligible Singapore entities, as part of Horizon Europe projects in priority
areas for Singapore.</p>
<p>NRF has organised the SG-HE Complementary Fund Information Sessions on
25 and 26 March 2026. Please refer to the following links for the <a href="https://go.gov.sg/sg-horizon-info-sessions" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">Information Session Slides</a> and
<a href="https://drive.google.com/file/d/1m3DCy-9nnXGGaVD7A23WOYnt6J2dbpQo/view?usp=sharing" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Video Recording</a>.</p>
<p>Further information on the Call for Applications for the SG-HE Complementary
Fund will be made available on the <a href="https://www.apply.gov.sg/grants/research" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Research Grant Portal (RGP)</a> in
April 2026.</p>
<p><strong>Please sign up </strong><a href="https://go.gov.sg/sghe" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">here</a><strong> to indicate your interest in the SG-HE Complementary Fund, and to receive notifications regarding the launch of the Call for Applications via e-mail.</strong>
</p>
<p>For further enquiries, please contact <strong><a rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">NCP@nrf.gov.sg</a>.</strong>
</p>
